How to Know When to Hire a CTO

Many startups face the critical decision of when to bring in a Chief Technology Officer (CTO). At Prepepper, we specialize in offering CTO as a Service for startups not ready to hire someone full-time. Our experts provide the strategic insight and leadership necessary to ensure your tech aligns with your business goals.

Signs that it might be time to bring in a CTO include:

  • Rapid growth or scale that needs proper technical infrastructure
  • New product launches requiring complex integrations
  • Increased technical challenges that are slowing down growth
